Pulse Chat

A workplace instant messaging app for communicating with your team in real time.

Getting Started

  1. Click on a conversation in the sidebar to open it
  2. Type your message in the input field at the bottom
  3. Press Enter or click Send to send your message
  4. Teammates may reply automatically during the simulation

Features

  • Unread message badges appear on conversations with new messages
  • A typing indicator shows when someone is composing a reply
  • Messages are timestamped for context

Tips

  • Press ESC to deselect the current conversation
  • Your conversation history is saved in your browser and persists across refreshes
  • Use chat to coordinate with teammates — your messages may trigger events in other apps
